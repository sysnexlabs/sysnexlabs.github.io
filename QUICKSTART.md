## Deploy
1. Create a public repo named **sysnex-labs.github.io**.
2. Copy the contents of the `website/` folder into the repo root.
3. Commit & push. GitHub Pages will publish automatically: https://sysnex-labs.github.io

## Customize
- Replace `assets/hero.png` with your preferred hero image.
- Edit colors in `style.css` (variables at the top).
- Update About/Contact text as needed.
