Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and purchase orders for enterprise customers. Contact us for payment terms and discount options.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Essential tier is completely free forever. You can also request a 30-day trial of any paid tier to evaluate all features.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer educational discounts for students, faculty, and academic institutions. Contact us with your educational email address for details.

What's included in support?

Essential includes community support. Standard includes email support with 48-hour response. Platform and Automotive include premium support with 24-hour response and dedicated account management.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Yes! All tiers can be used for commercial projects. The Essential tier is free for both personal and commercial use. Paid tiers include commercial licensing.
