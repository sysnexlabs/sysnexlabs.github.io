# Website Review & Improvement Plan
## Benchmark: Dassault Systèmes Professional Standards

**Reference**: https://www.3ds.com/de/
**Date**: January 2026

---

## Key Professional Elements from Dassault Systèmes Website

### 1. **Clear Value Proposition**
- Bold headline: "Wir sind die 3DEXPERIENCE Company"
- Subtitle: "Virtual Worlds for Real Life™"
- Direct, memorable messaging

### 2. **Customer Success Stories**
- **EVUM Motors**: Automotive case study with specific outcomes
- **Nowy Styl**: Furniture manufacturing transformation
- **Brose**: Automotive supplier collaboration
- **Geberit**: Sanitary products global collaboration
- **Robot at Work**: Production optimization

**Format**: Each includes:
- Company name and industry
- Challenge/situation
- Solution implemented
- Outcomes/results
- "Mehr erfahren" (Learn more) link

### 3. **Industry-Focused Sections**
- Clear industry categorization:
  - Industrielle Fertigung (Industrial Manufacturing)
  - Life Sciences und Gesundheitswesen (Life Sciences & Healthcare)
  - Infrastruktur & Stadtplanung (Infrastructure & Urban Planning)

### 4. **Platform Messaging**
- "Eine Plattform, endlose Möglichkeiten" (One Platform, Endless Possibilities)
- Emphasis on unified platform approach
- Clear platform benefits

### 5. **Professional Structure**
- Hero → Value Prop → Industries → Customers → Platform → Products → CTAs
- Logical flow from problem to solution to proof
- Strong visual hierarchy

### 6. **Trust Indicators**
- Customer logos
- Metrics and statistics
- Case studies with real companies
- Professional imagery

## Current SysNex Website Analysis

### ✅ Strengths
1. Modern design with animations
2. Clear product differentiation (Syscribe vs CodeFlow)
3. Good technical content
4. Interactive demo (Try Yourself)
5. Theme support (dark/light)

### ⚠️ Areas for Improvement

#### 1. **Hero Section**
**Current**: Generic "Model-Based Systems Engineering, reimagined"
**Should be**: More direct, platform-focused value proposition
**Example**: "Wir sind die SysMLv2 CodeFlow Platform" or similar bold statement

#### 2. **Missing Customer Success Stories**
**Current**: No customer testimonials or case studies
**Should add**: 
- Customer testimonials section
- Case studies with specific outcomes
- Customer logos (if available)
- Real-world application examples

#### 3. **Industry Focus**
**Current**: Generic benefits, no industry-specific sections
**Should add**:
- Automotive/Automotive Safety section
- Aerospace/Defense section
- Medical Devices section
- General Systems Engineering section

#### 4. **Platform Messaging**
**Current**: CodeFlow mentioned but not emphasized as unified platform
**Should enhance**: 
- Clear platform value proposition
- "One Platform, Multiple Solutions" messaging
- Platform benefits prominently featured

#### 5. **Content Flow**
**Current**: Good but could be more logical
**Recommended flow**:
1. Hero (Value Prop)
2. Platform Overview (CodeFlow)
3. Industry Solutions (Automotive, Aerospace, Medical)
4. Customer Success Stories
5. Product Details (Syscribe + Commercial)
6. Technical Features
7. CTA

#### 6. **Professional Polish**
- Add more visual trust indicators
- Improve section headers (more descriptive)
- Better use of whitespace
- Professional imagery suggestions
